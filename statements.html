STATEMENTS

My work emerges from a place of inner silence and unpremeditated response. Each painting begins without preconception—through meditative automatism, layers of color and form arise as heart-images from the unconscious.

The process is one of surrender: allowing vitality to surface naturally, as it does in nature. Translucent veils overlap, revealing and concealing, echoing the fleeting rhythms of life and the quiet depth beneath.

These are not illustrations of emotion, but direct traces of inner movement—fragments of something felt but not named. I seek to hold the tension between presence and absence, light and shadow, the seen and the unseen.

Ultimately, the paintings invite the viewer to pause, to meet their own inner depths in the space between seeing and feeling.
